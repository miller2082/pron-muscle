Rules for Practicing English Pronunciation

  1. Syllable Identification
  2. Face Position
  3. Sound (Vocalize)
  4. Release to a Vowel

Rules for Pronouncing R & W

  1. Face Position: Pucker
  2. No Tongue!

Rules for Pronouncing L & TH

  1. Face Position Fake Smile
  2. Jaw Open
  3. Tongue SOFTLY Touches Teeth
  4. L: Vocalize Only
  5. Th: Exhale Air Between Teeth
  6. Th: Vibration and SOFT 'Feather' Touch

Rules for Pronouncing F & V

  1. Face Position: 'Bite' Bottom Lip
  2. F: Create air Pressure
  3. F Is Explosive, Like a Popped Baloon
  4. V: SOFTLY touch Bottom Lip to Top Teeth
  5. V: Never Touch Top Lip to Bottom Lip

Identifying Syllables Makes Pronunciation Easy!

  1. Sound Out Syllables
  2. Each Syllable Gets One Beat
  3. 1/2 Beat Between Syllables: Time for Face Position Change!
  4. Find the Rhythm of the Words and Language
  5. Speak with the Rhythm of the Language
  6. Listen with the Rhythm of the Language!

英語の筋トレ！ and “Mastering the R Sound” 英語 R の発音をマスターしよう!

  4. “R”の発音は難しいですか？

上記の質問に一つでもあてはまるようなら、発音のトレーニングをやってみましょう。

簡単なルールを覚えれば、難しい発音もできるようになります。

英語を正しく発音するための　”筋トレ“　を伝授します。
